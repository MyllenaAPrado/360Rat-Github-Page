360Rat

Tool to annotate spatially regions of interest for omnidirectional visual data in EPR. The annotation on the images follows the distortions of the pole and the continuity of the edges. With the software you can see the perpsctive view while you are anottated. You also can watch the video during the annotation.

Developed using Equirectangular-toolbox, OpenCV, PyQt5 5.15.4, and python 3.9.5.
